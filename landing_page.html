About SRM University

SRM University AP (Andhra Pradesh) is a private university located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. The university was established in 2017 and is a part of the SRM Group, which has a presence in the field of education for over 50 years. SRM University AP offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as engineering, management, law, basic sciences, and liberal arts. The university has state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities such as research labs, libraries, sports complex, auditorium, and hostel.

Lifestyle at SRM AP

SRM AP (SRM University, Andhra Pradesh) is a university that aims to provide a holistic education experience for its students. As such, the university encourages its students to adopt a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Here are some aspects of the lifestyle at SRM AP:

    Physical activity:
    The university has several sports facilities, including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, and tennis court. Students are encouraged to participate in sports and engage in physical activities to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
    Healthy eating:
    The university has a canteen that provides healthy and nutritious food options for students. The canteen also has a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to different dietary preferences.
    Cultural activities:
    SRM AP encourages students to participate in cultural activities like music, dance, and drama. These activities not only provide a creative outlet but also help in developing social skills and confidence.
    Academic rigor:
    SRM AP places a strong emphasis on academic rigor and encourages students to maintain a balanced lifestyle by managing their time effectively. This helps students to avoid burnout and maintain good mental health.
Overall, SRM AP strives to create an environment that supports the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of its students.
